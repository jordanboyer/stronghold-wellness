THE PROBLEM

Most programs weren't built for your body

You've done the group classes. Downloaded the app. Maybe followed a program from someone on Instagram. And it kind of worked — until it didn't.

The issue isn't effort. It's that no one assessed how you actually move before handing you a program. Your tight hips, your limited rotation, the shoulder that nags on overhead presses — those things matter. And every generic program ignores them.

The Tailored Approach starts where most programs skip: a live movement screen that identifies what your body can and can't do right now. Then I build around that — not despite it.
